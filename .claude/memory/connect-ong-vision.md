Connect ONG ("Plataforma de Doação / Conecta ORG") is a digital platform that intermediates between **doadores** (pessoas físicas E jurídicas/empresas) and **ONGs**, to make donation simple, transparent and trackable. TCC at COTIL/Will's class (advisor site: https://willcotil.github.io/aulas2026/). Team: Gabriel Chinelatto (203161), Luan Felipe (203083), Abner Viola (203223), Arthur de Jesus (203076).

**Critical mandate from the user:** from now on treat this as a **real, professional product** — not just a school project. Focus on UX, security, scalability, maintainability, social impact, market viability. There is a **prize** for the best project at the September showcase (judged by industry professionals — company owners/employees). The user wants complex, polished, professional features and trusts me to drive the direction.

**Role model clarification:** "Empresa" is a *kind of doador* (pessoa jurídica), NOT a separate role. The standardized user roles are **DOADOR** and **ONG** (a doador may later be flagged PF/PJ). This confirms the earlier decision to standardize desktop away from a separate "EMPRESA" role. See [[connect-ong-architecture]].

Problem: ONGs struggle to raise resources, publicize specific needs, communicate with donors, and prove transparency. Donors struggle to find trustworthy ONGs, see real needs, track impact, and stay in contact. The product's north star is closing that gap. See [[connect-ong-banca-feedback]], [[connect-ong-milestones]], [[connect-ong-tech-guidelines]].
